What is Data Dove?

Data Dove is a premium private Discord group dedicated to ensuring its members the purchase of new and upcoming hyped products as soon as they are released and available. Using bots and insider information, members are able to prepare themselves and use programs to checkout as soon as an item drops.


Tasks: As a completely new group, Data Dove had a solid grasp on how the group would function, but had no designs for their logo and website. As an online based premium group, it was mandatory to have a great logo and easy-to-use website since most members would be viewing them daily.

Logo Design

With a name like Data Dove, it was obvious to go with a bird-like design for the main logo. Using the word “data” and Data Dove being an online somewhat-tech group, I decided to go with a low poly-esque direction for the bird. I believe low poly graphics are widely used in tech related designs and that felt like a great way to represent the “data” part of Data Dove. I used a high contrast of dark navy and bright pink to bring a neon tech vibe to the logo.

Website Design & Development

For the website, I went with the same color scheme as the logo but added more complementary colors and shades to give a more diverse and vibrant look. Similarly, I kept the dark neon tech vibe throughout the whole website and made sure every component blended well with each other to create a smooth website look and experience. I kept in mind the user experience and mobile responsiveness as I developed my design into a functioning website.
